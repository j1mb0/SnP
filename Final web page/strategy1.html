GOOD LUCK DUDE

Do not let the map intimidate you, let us walk you through it. Your goal is to plant and pick as many crops as possible, in order to raise enough money to beat Monsanto!

  • Start game by clicking on the map
  • Click around your BASE to plant crops
  • After 15 seconds, you can click on the crops that are now GOLD and earn money
  • Earn enough money to send protesters on Monsanto’s side to keep him from planting or picking up any of his crops for 15 seconds, that gives you time to lead ahead.
  • Continue until you have earned $5,000 before your opponent
